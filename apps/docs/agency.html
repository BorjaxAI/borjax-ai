White-Label Setup

Agency accounts can customize the BorjaxAI interface with your own branding, presented to your clients as your own AI product.

Upload your brand assets
In the Agency dashboard, go to Settings → Branding. Upload your logo (SVG or PNG, min 200×50px), set your primary color hex, and configure your brand name.
Configure a custom domain
Add a CNAME DNS record pointing your subdomain (e.g., ai.youragency.com) to app.borjaxai.com. Then enter your domain in Settings → Custom Domain and we'll provision an SSL certificate automatically.
Set a custom system prompt
Configure a global system prompt that will be prepended to all Claude interactions in your workspace. This lets you define your AI's persona, constraints, and specialized knowledge.
Share with clients
Send your custom domain to clients. They'll see your branding — not BorjaxAI. Create client workspaces to isolate each client's conversations, agents, and token usage.

Client Workspace Management

Client workspaces are isolated environments under your Agency account. Each workspace has its own:

  • Token pool (allocated from your 2M monthly quota)
  • Conversation history (private to that workspace)
  • Custom agents and task history
  • Login credentials (managed separately from your team)
bash
# Create a client workspace via API
curl -X POST https://api.borjaxai.com/v1/agency/workspaces \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_AGENCY_TOKEN" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "name": "Acme Corp",
    "client_email": "ai-admin@acme.com",
    "token_allocation": 200000
  }'
ℹ️
Token allocation: You can allocate up to your total monthly quota across client workspaces. Unallocated tokens remain in your main workspace pool. Allocations can be adjusted at any time.

Team Seats

Add up to 10 team members to your Agency account. Each team member gets their own login and can be assigned one of two roles:

RolePermissions
AdminFull access: billing, branding, workspace management, team management, all features
MemberAccess to chat, tasks, and agents. Cannot access billing, branding settings, or manage other team members.

Invite team members from the Agency dashboard under Settings → Team. Invitations are sent by email and expire after 48 hours.

Billing

The Agency plan is billed at $99/month on a recurring monthly subscription via Stripe. You can view and download invoices from Settings → Billing.

  • Upgrades from Pro take effect immediately with prorated billing
  • Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle
  • No setup fees, no long-term contracts — cancel anytime
  • For custom volume or annual contracts, contact us below
